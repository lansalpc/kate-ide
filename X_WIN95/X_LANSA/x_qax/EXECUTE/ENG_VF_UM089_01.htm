Grouping business objects

Step 4. Enter some application (/group) names below (separated by commas):

Business objects are grouped together to make security easier to manage and end user access simpler and more manageable.
These types of business object groupings are named many different things (eg: Applications, Systems, Subsystems, etc).

For an example, an ERP system might have its business objects logically grouped together into applications or subsystems named :
- Human Resources, Ordering, Dispatch, Accounts, Inventory, Production
a Banking system might have its business objects logically grouped together into applications or subsystems named :
- Customer Accounts, Lending, Marketing .

Don't organise business objects by user role - this can be done later.
