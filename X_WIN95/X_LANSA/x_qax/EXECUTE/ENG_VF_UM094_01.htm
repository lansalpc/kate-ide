Now you can try out the look and feel of your design.
Click on one of your business objects (in the tree view on the left).

When a user clicks on this business object they will use the filter to select the business objects they want to work with.

You can emulate this by using the "Emulate Search" button in the filter.

The user will then click on an entry in the instance list (on the right of the filter).
This will cause the Actions for this business object to be displayed.
